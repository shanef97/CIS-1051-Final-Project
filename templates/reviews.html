Temple Teppanyaki


Located just across from the SERC on 12th St, Temple Teppanyaki is a Japanese-Korean fusion truck serving delicious dishes such as tonkatsu, rice bowls, teppanyaki, and the fan-favorite #9 Wings with sticky rice pictured above.

The truck can get busy around lunchtime, so we recommend calling ahead for pickup, especially if you're ordering the #9 Wings. Call ahead at (267) 342-3190. The truck is open from 9AM - 5:30PM Monday - Friday, and accepts Cash, Card, and Apple Pay.
