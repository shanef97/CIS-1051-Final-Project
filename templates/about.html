About Us


Cherry Eats is a student-run blog focused on food trucks and restaurants on and around Temple Univeristy's campus. Covering everything from humble food carts to full-scale restaurants, we aim to give students and local residents a curated list of the best food in the Temple area.

Temple is among the best universities in the nation in terms of its food scene, and new food trucks and restaurants are constantly being opened and reviewed by our team. Make Cherry Eats your trusted advisor for all things Temple food related!
